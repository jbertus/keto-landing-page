Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Keto Contractor ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. What We Collect

We collect your email address when you submit a form on our website (such as requesting the Sunday Prep Method guide) or when you create an account on the Keto Contractor app.

When you use the app, we may also collect usage data such as meal plans, recipes generated, and progress entries. This data is tied to your account and used only to provide the service.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your email address to send you the content you requested and occasional updates about Keto Contractor. We do not sell your email address or share it with third parties for marketing purposes.

App data (meals, progress, recipes) is used solely to power the features of the app and is never sold or shared.

3. Email Communications

By submitting your email on our website, you agree to receive the requested content and may receive follow-up emails from Keto Contractor. You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email or by emailing us at support@ketocontractor.com with the subject "Unsubscribe."

4. Cookies

Our website does not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics. The app may use session storage to preserve your preferences within a browser session — this data is never transmitted to us.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate:

Resend — email delivery. Your email address is transmitted to Resend solely for the purpose of sending you the email you requested.

Supabase — database and authentication for the app.

Whop — subscription billing and payment processing. Whop's privacy policy governs any data collected during checkout.

Netlify — website hosting.

6. Data Retention

We retain your email address and app data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal information we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at support@ketocontractor.com.
