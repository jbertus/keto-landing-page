Eat This Much vs Keto Contractor: Any-Diet Autopilot vs Type 2 Autopilot

Eat This Much is an automatic meal planner - set calories and diet style, get a generated plan and grocery list. It's genuinely useful for general meal planning across many diets.

Where Eat This Much is strong: Flexible automatic planning for any diet and solid grocery-list generation. As a general-purpose planning robot, it's clever.

Where it leaves a type 2 diabetic on their own: General-purpose is the problem. It optimizes calories and macros for whoever you tell it to be - it doesn't understand a type 2 diagnosis, doesn't do restaurants at all, and can't look at a plate or a menu. Keto Contractor does the same auto-planning but built around net carbs and diabetes, then follows you out the front door to the restaurant.
