Keto at Cracker Barrel Old Country Store: Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

38 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
Cracker Barrel can work for keto if you focus on explicitly grilled proteins, bunless sandwiches, eggs with breakfast meat, green beans, broccoli, and salads without croutons or sweet toppings. Avoid the many Southern comfort staples built from flour, breading, dumplings, pancakes, biscuits, potatoes, mac and cheese, sweet drinks, and desserts. For entrées, always say no biscuits/cornbread and replace starchy sides with green beans, broccoli, or a plain house salad.
