Keto at Taco Bell: Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

37 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
Taco Bell can be made keto only by stripping away tortillas, taco shells, rice, beans, potatoes, chips, crunchy strips, and sugary drinks. The safest food orders are no-shell taco bowls, the Power Menu Bowl with no rice/beans, or meat-and-cheese fillings from burritos/quesadillas served in a bowl. Avoid combo boxes, nachos, Mexican Pizza, bean/rice/potato items, sweets, and Freeze drinks because they are too starch- or sugar-heavy.
