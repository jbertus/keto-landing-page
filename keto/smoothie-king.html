Keto at Smoothie King: Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

14 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
Smoothie King is difficult for strict keto because most smoothies are fruit- or sugar-based, and the carbs are blended into the drink rather than removable. Stick to Keto Champ options or plain/base Gladiator options in the smallest size, with no turbinado, fruit, juice, frozen yogurt, honey, or sweet add-ins.
