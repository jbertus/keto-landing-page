Keto at The Prime Rib: Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

74 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
The Prime Rib is very keto-friendly if you order steak, seafood, raw shellfish, or simple protein add-ons and swap the included potato/fries for asparagus or another non-starchy vegetable. Avoid the standalone starch sides, mac/risotto/potatoes/fries, fried appetizers, port, and the sweet dessert-style cocktails. Best strict-keto order: steak or blackened red snapper with asparagus plus béarnaise, chimichurri, or butter.
