Keto at P.F. Chang's: Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

37 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
P.F. Chang's can be done keto only by treating entrées as protein-and-vegetable plates: no rice, no noodles, and sauces light or on the side. The best choices are Black Pepper Filet, beef/chicken with broccoli, Pepper Steak, and carefully modified salads or green vegetable sides. Avoid sushi, noodles, fried rice, dumplings, wontons, egg rolls, crispy/honey/orange/sweet-and-sour items, and anything tempura or crispy.
