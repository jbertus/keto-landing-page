Keto at Panda Express: Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

21 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
Panda Express is challenging for strict keto because most entrées use sweet sauces, breading, rice, or noodles. The safest strategy is to build a Bowl or Plate with Super Greens and a lower-carb protein such as Grilled Teriyaki Chicken with no sauce, Black Pepper steak, Mushroom Chicken, String Bean Chicken Breast, Broccoli Beef, or Black Pepper Chicken with light sauce. Avoid rice, chow mein, rangoons, egg rolls, Orange Chicken, Beijing Beef, Honey Walnut Shrimp, sweet drinks, and any crispy or honey-glazed item.
