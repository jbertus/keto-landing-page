Keto at Outback Steakhouse: Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

80 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
Outback is very workable for keto if you build meals around steak, grilled chicken, grilled shrimp, salmon, lobster, wings in Buffalo/naked flavors, and non-starchy sides like broccoli, asparagus, or salad. The main traps are Bloomin' items, coconut shrimp, pasta, fries/potatoes/rice/mac and cheese, sweet BBQ/teriyaki/glazes, desserts, lemonade, soda, and cocktails. For a strict 20g/day keto target, order grilled protein with broccoli/asparagus and keep sauces on the side.
