Keto at Walk-On's Sports Bistreaux - Shreveport Restaurant: Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

47 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
Walk-On's can work for keto if you focus on blackened/grilled proteins, bunless burgers, grilled chicken substitutions, bone-in wings with buffalo or lemon pepper, broccoli, and side salads. Avoid the fried seafood/chicken, pasta, rice, grits, fries, hush puppies, sweet glazes, and sugary cocktails; most keto orders need a clear "no bun/no rice/no fries/no sweet sauce" request.
