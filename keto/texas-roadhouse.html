Keto at Texas Roadhouse: Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

46 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
Texas Roadhouse can work well for keto if you focus on steaks, grilled salmon, grilled chicken, pork chops, and shrimp while skipping rolls, rice, potatoes, fries, and sweet BBQ sauce. Fried appetizers, Chicken Critters, country-fried entrées, desserts, margaritas, juices, and sugary specialty drinks should be avoided for a 20g/day carb goal.
