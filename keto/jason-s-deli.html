Keto at Jason's Deli: Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

78 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
Jason's Deli can work for keto if you treat most sandwiches, clubs, wraps, muffalettas, and catering boxes as lettuce wraps or bowls and remove bread, tortillas, chips, fruit, and sweet sides. Best bets are deli meats, tuna salad with eggs, chef/club/Caesar-style salads with no croutons, steamed veggies, and black coffee or zero-sugar drinks. Avoid the pasta, potatoes, pizzas, desserts, fruit trays, sugary drinks, and standalone starchy sides.
