Keto at Arby's: Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

36 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
Arby's can work for keto mainly by ordering roast beef, ham, turkey, gyro, Reuben, or cheesesteak items without the bun, roll, pita, or bread and skipping fries and sugary drinks. Avoid the crispy chicken/tenders, fries, mac and cheese, fried snacks, desserts, lemonades, juice, milk, and shakes because the breading, starch, or sugar cannot be removed.
