Keto at Golden Corral Buffet & Grill: Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

26 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
Golden Corral can work for keto if you build plates around clearly low-carb proteins like sirloin steak, carved turkey, baked fish, eggs, bacon, sausage, pot roast, and bunless steakburger patties. Pair them with broccoli, green beans, Brussels sprouts, cabbage, mushrooms, peppers, or a carefully built salad, and avoid the many buffet starches, fried/breaded foods, desserts, fruit, juices, rolls, potatoes, rice, pasta, and sweet sauces.
