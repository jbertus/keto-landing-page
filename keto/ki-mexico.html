Keto at Ki' Mexico: Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

77 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
Ki' Mexico can work for keto if you order tacos and tortugas as bowls with no tortilla or bread, and skip rice, beans, corn, chips, tostadas, and honey vinaigrette. The best food choices are Bisté, Chiquitibum, Taco de Camaron, Pescado Borracho, Gringa, Quesabrosa without tortillas, and salads with chicken or shrimp. For drinks, stick to water, straight tequila/mezcal/liquor, or one dry wine; avoid the sweet cocktails, sodas, juices, root beer, and most beers.
