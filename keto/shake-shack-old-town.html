Keto at Shake Shack Old Town: Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

51 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
The best keto strategy at Shake Shack Old Town is to order beef burgers or flat-top dogs lettuce-wrapped or bunless, skipping ketchup and sweet sauces. Avoid fries, shakes, custard, lemonades, fried chicken items, and the fried 'Shroom/veggie-based items unless the fried component can be removed from a beef burger stack.
