Keto at Copeland's of New Orleans: Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

46 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
Copeland's can work for keto if you focus on raw/chargrilled oysters, steaks, blackened or grilled fish, grilled chicken, salads, and non-starchy sides. The main strategy is to remove rice, pasta, potatoes, fries, bread, toast, sweet sauces, and breading; use spinach, broccoli, creamed spinach, squash succotash, or side salad as replacements. Avoid the fried seafood plates, gumbo/étouffée over rice, pasta unless converted to squash noodles, and standalone starchy sides.
