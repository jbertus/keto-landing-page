Keto at Hooters: Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

34 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
Hooters can work for keto if you focus on steamed shrimp, snow crab legs, naked or smoked bone-in wings with low-carb sauces, bunless burgers, bunless Philly-style sandwiches, and salads without croutons. Avoid the breaded items---original wings, boneless wings, tenders, Buffalo shrimp, fried fish---as well as fries, tots, nachos, desserts, and sugary drinks.
