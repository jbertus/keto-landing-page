Keto at Pizza Hut: Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

2 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
Pizza Hut is very limited for strict keto because the visible menu is mostly pizza crust, melts, pasta, breaded boneless wings, bread sides, and desserts. Based only on the provided menu text, the safest keto choices are Pepsi Zero and cream cheese dip without any bread or crust; avoid the dough-, pasta-, breaded-, and dessert-based items.
