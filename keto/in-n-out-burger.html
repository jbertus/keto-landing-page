Keto at In-N-Out Burger: Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

25 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
Keto at In-N-Out is very doable if you order burgers Protein Style, skip the bun, and avoid spread or keep it very light. The best choices are Flying Dutchman, Double-Double Protein Style, 3×3/4×4 Protein Style, and unsweetened drinks; avoid all fries, shakes, lemonade, sweet tea, and sugary sodas.
