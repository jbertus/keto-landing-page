Keto at Del Taco: Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

36 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
Keto at Del Taco is possible by turning grilled chicken, carne asada, seasoned beef tacos, burgers, and some burritos into no-shell/no-tortilla bowls. Avoid fried or battered proteins, fries, hashbrowns, churros, shakes, lemonade, and chip-based nachos unless the chips are completely removed and a real protein remains.
