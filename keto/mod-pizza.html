Keto at MOD Pizza: Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

15 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
MOD Pizza is keto-friendly mainly if you skip pizza entirely and order a salad with low-carb toppings. Best choices are create-your-own salads, Caesar without croutons, Italian Chop without chickpeas, and zero-sugar drinks or water. Avoid all pizzas, cheesy bread, desserts, kids pizza meals, and sugary bottled beverages.
