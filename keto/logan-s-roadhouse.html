Keto at Logan's Roadhouse: Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

85 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
Logan's Roadhouse can work well for keto if you build meals around mesquite-grilled steaks, grilled chicken, salmon, shrimp, pork chops, ribs with sauce on the side, and non-starchy sides like broccoli, green beans, Caesar salad, garden salad, wedge salad, or grilled zucchini. Avoid the rolls, fries, rice, potatoes, corn, pasta, breaded chicken/fish/shrimp, desserts, sweet cocktails, lemonade, juice, and regular soda. For burgers and grilled sandwiches, order no bun and no fries; for salads, remove croutons, tortilla strips, corn/beans, cranberries, and sweet dressings.
