Keto at NEE HAO | CHINESE & SUSHI BAR: Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

100 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
Best keto choices at NEE HAO are sashimi, Naruto cucumber-wrapped rolls, baked salmon, steamed protein-and-vegetable dishes, and Chinese stir-fries ordered with no rice and sauce light/on the side. Avoid rice, noodles, fried rolls, tempura, sweet-and-sour, General Tso's, orange, sesame, eel sauce, teriyaki sauce, and sugary drinks. For a 7g net-carb meal cap, be strict: no rice at all, no crunchy toppings, and use soy sauce, hot sauce, spicy mayo, or very light savory sauce only.
