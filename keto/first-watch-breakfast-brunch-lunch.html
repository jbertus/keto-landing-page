Keto at First Watch | Breakfast • Brunch • Lunch: Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

50 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
First Watch can work for keto if you build around eggs, omelets, frittatas, Benedicts without bread, breakfast meats, avocado, and salads without toast or sweet dressing. The main pattern is to remove toast, tortillas, buns, potatoes, quinoa, beans, corn, fruit, honey/sweet sauces, and sugary drinks. Best simple orders are omelets with no toast, Benedicts with no ciabatta, The Traditional with no toast/potatoes, or Cobb Salad with no ciabatta.
