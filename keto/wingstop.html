Keto at Wingstop: Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

21 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
Wingstop can work for keto if you stick to classic bone-in wings, avoid boneless wings/tenders/sandwiches, and choose low-carb flavors like Plain, Lemon Pepper, Garlic Parmesan, Original Hot, Atomic, Cajun, Louisiana Rub, or Mild. Skip fries, corn, sugary drinks, desserts, honey mustard, and sweet sauces; pair wings with veggie sticks plus ranch or blue cheese.
