Keto at Sonic Drive-In: Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

19 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
Keto at Sonic is mostly bunless burgers, bunless hot dogs, and breakfast fillings ordered without the tortilla or toast. Avoid the fried chicken items, fries/tots, onion rings, mozzarella sticks, desserts, slushes, limeades, juice, and regular soft drinks; combos only work if you skip the fries/tots and choose an unsweetened drink.
