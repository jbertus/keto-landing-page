Keto at Dunkin': Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

42 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
Dunkin' can work for keto mainly with black coffee, plain espresso/Americano, unsweetened tea, water, zero-sugar bottled drinks, bacon, and breakfast sandwich fillings ordered without bread or wraps. Avoid the donuts, Munchkins, muffins, bagels, croissants, hash browns, frozen drinks, refreshers, juices, sweet lattes, and all flavor swirls because those are sugar/starch-heavy and not removable enough for a 12g net-carb meal cap.
