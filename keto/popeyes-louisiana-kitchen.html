Keto at Popeyes Louisiana Kitchen: Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

37 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
Popeyes is a difficult keto restaurant because most chicken, sandwiches, tenders, shrimp, biscuits, and sides are fried, breaded, sugary, or starchy. The best keto strategy is blackened tenders or bone-in wings with low-carb sauces, while skipping all fries, biscuits, mac, beans, mashed potatoes, coleslaw, lemonade, sweet tea, and regular soda.
