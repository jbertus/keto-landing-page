Keto at McDonald's: Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

36 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
McDonald's can work for keto mainly by ordering beef burgers without the bun and without ketchup, or breakfast sandwiches without the biscuit, muffin, bagel, or McGriddles cakes. Avoid all McCrispy items, McNuggets, Filet-O-Fish, fries, hash browns, desserts, smoothies, frappés, and sweet sauces because those carbs are not removable enough for a 12g net-carb meal.
