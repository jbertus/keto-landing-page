Keto at Firehouse Subs Shreveport: Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

80 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
Firehouse Subs can work for keto if you treat subs and wraps as meat-and-cheese bowls: no bread, no tortilla, and skip sweet sauces like BBQ, honey mustard, honey chipotle, cranberry, and sweet slaw. Best choices are grilled chicken, steak, brisket without BBQ, deli-meat bowls, chopped salads with low-sugar dressing, pickles, side salad, unsweet tea, water, or diet/zero drinks. Avoid meatball/marinara subs, mac and cheese, fries, chips, cookies, brownies, sugary drinks, and catering platters as served.
