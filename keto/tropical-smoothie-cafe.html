Keto at Tropical Smoothie Cafe: Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

23 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
Tropical Smoothie Cafe is difficult for strict keto because smoothies, bowls, fruit, tortillas, flatbreads, rice, beans, and sweet toppings dominate the menu. The best approach is to order grilled-chicken, egg, turkey, bacon, sausage, and cheese items as bowls with no tortilla/flatbread/bread, and skip rice, beans, wontons, fruit, honey, and sugary drinks.
