Keto at Twin Peaks: Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

41 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
Twin Peaks can work for keto if you focus on steaks, grilled/blackened proteins, smoked or naked bone-in wings, bunless burgers, and salads with croutons/sweet dressings removed. Avoid the fried appetizers, flatbreads, potatoes, fries, mac & cheese, sweet wing sauces, and desserts. For a strict 20g daily carb goal, the safest orders are steak with asparagus/broccoli, Smoked & Grilled wings with Lemon Pepper or Garlic Parmesan, or a Caesar salad with grilled chicken, salmon, or shrimp and no croutons.
