Keto at Hardee's: Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

34 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
Hardee's can work for keto mainly by ordering charbroiled burgers without buns and skipping fries, Hash Rounds, biscuits, tortillas, and sugary drinks. Breakfast is possible if you turn biscuit sandwiches into bowls, but avoid all hand-breaded chicken, tenders, French toast, desserts, and standalone potato sides.
