Keto at Jersey Mike's Subs: Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

27 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
Jersey Mike's is very workable for keto because nearly every cold sub or hot cheese steak can be ordered as a Sub in a Tub or bowl with no bread. Prioritize meat, cheese, bacon, oil, vinegar, mayo, ranch, lettuce, and peppers; avoid chips, cookies, brownies, soda, sweet drinks, and sugary sauces like hot honey.
