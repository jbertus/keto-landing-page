Keto at Noodles and Company: Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

39 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
Noodles & Company is challenging for strict keto because most entrées are built around noodles, mac, tortelloni, ramen, rice noodles, or bread. The safest orders are standalone grilled chicken, steak, shrimp, pulled pork, Lemon Parmesan Broccoli, Caesar Salad without croutons, or Chicken Caesar Salad without croutons. Some protein noodle bowls can work only if the restaurant can truly omit the noodles and sweet sauces, leaving the protein and vegetables.
