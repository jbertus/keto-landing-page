Keto at Jack in the Box: Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

68 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
Jack in the Box can work for keto mainly by ordering burgers or breakfast sandwiches with no bun, biscuit, croissant, sourdough, tortilla, or waffle, and by skipping fries/hash browns. The safest meals are bunless beef burgers, bunless breakfast meat-and-egg items, grilled chicken items without bread, side salad, water, black coffee, or confirmed unsweet iced tea. Avoid nuggets, crispy strips, fries, egg rolls, poppers, desserts, shakes, juice, smoothies, and any chicken sandwich not explicitly listed as grilled.
