Keto at Texas de Brazil - Baton Rouge: Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

31 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
Texas de Brazil is very keto-friendly if you focus on the flame-grilled meats and choose non-starchy sides like sautéed mushrooms or salads with low-carb dressing. The main hazards are the included Brazilian cheese bread, rice & beans, potatoes, fries, fried bananas, grain salads, desserts, and sugary sodas, all of which should be skipped.
