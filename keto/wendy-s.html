Keto at Wendy's: Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

44 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
Wendy's keto strategy is mostly bunless beef burgers, grilled chicken wraps ordered without the tortilla, salads with crunchy/sweet carbs removed, and zero-sugar drinks. Avoid the crispy chicken, nuggets, fries, baked potatoes, Frostys, bakery items, lemonades, sweet teas, and regular sugary drinks. Wendy's chili contains beans, so keep chili-based items light if you use them at all.
