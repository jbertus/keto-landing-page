Keto at Starbucks Coffee Company: Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

42 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
Starbucks can work for keto if you stick to unsweetened coffee or tea, or modify espresso drinks by removing syrups/sauces and replacing milk with heavy cream diluted with water. For food, the best options are breakfast sandwiches or paninis eaten without bread/wraps; avoid baked goods, oatmeal, Frappuccinos, refreshers, lemonade drinks, and desserts.
