Keto at Applebee's Grill + Bar: Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

63 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
Applebee's can work for keto if you focus on steak, grilled chicken, blackened salmon, bone-in wings, bunless burgers, and salads with crunchy/sweet toppings removed. The biggest traps are fries, potatoes, pasta, breadsticks, tortillas, boneless wings, chicken tenders, sweet glazes, sugary cocktails, and desserts. For a ≤12g net-carb meal, order protein plus broccoli, green beans, or a no-crouton side salad.
