Keto at Buffalo Wild Wings: Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

67 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
Buffalo Wild Wings can work well for keto if you focus on traditional bone-in wings, Naked Tenders, bunless burgers, grilled salads, and zero-sugar drinks. The biggest traps are boneless wings, hand-breaded tenders, fries, tots, pretzels, nachos, mac & cheese, desserts, sugary drinks, and sweet sauces like Honey BBQ, Teriyaki, Asian Zing, Mango Habanero, and Sweet BBQ.
