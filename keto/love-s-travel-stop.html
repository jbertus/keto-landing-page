Keto at Love's Travel Stop: Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

12 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
Best keto choices at this Love's are bunless roller-grill meats, unsweet tea, black coffee, water, and carefully label-checked low-sugar jerky. Avoid taquitos, buns, pizza, corn dogs, candy, sweet tea, soda, and sugary coffee drinks. For any hot item, the safest rule is: remove bread/tortilla/bun and skip ketchup or sweet sauces.
