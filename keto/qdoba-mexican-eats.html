Keto at QDOBA Mexican Eats: Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

50 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
QDOBA can be very keto-friendly if you order bowls, salads, or tacos with no tortilla/shell, no rice, no beans, no chips, and no corn salsa. The safest build is lettuce plus a protein, fajita veggies, guac, cheese, queso, sour cream, and salsa; avoid burritos, quesadillas, nachos, rice, beans, potatoes, desserts, and sugary drinks.
