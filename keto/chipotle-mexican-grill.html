Keto at Chipotle Mexican Grill: Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

34 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
Chipotle can be very keto-friendly if you order bowls or salads and skip tortillas, taco shells, rice, beans, chips, and corn salsa. The best choices are meat-based bowls or salads with chicken, steak, barbacoa, or carnitas plus cheese, sour cream, queso, and/or guacamole. Avoid the sugary drinks, chips, and quesadilla because those are fundamentally high-carb items.
