Keto at Carrabba's Italian Grill: Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

47 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
Carrabba's can work for keto if you focus on Tuscan-grilled steaks, grilled chicken, salmon, seafood, and non-starchy sides like asparagus, broccoli, spinach, and salad. Avoid the pasta section, fried appetizers, Chicken Parmesan, breadsticks, fries, desserts, lemonade, and sangria; for sandwiches, remove the bread and skip starchy combo sides.
