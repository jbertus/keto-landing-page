Keto at Burger King: Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

88 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
Burger King can be keto if you stick to flame-grilled beef burgers ordered with no bun and no ketchup, and skip fries completely. Avoid all crispy chicken, nuggets, chicken fries, fish sandwiches, tots, fries, onion rings, desserts, shakes, juice, and regular soda. Best drinks are water, black BK Café coffee, or Diet Coke.
