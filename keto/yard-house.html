Keto at Yard House: Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

33 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
Yard House can work for keto if you focus on grilled steaks, grilled seafood, bunless burgers/sandwiches, and modified salads. The main strategy is to remove buns, tortillas, rice, mashed potatoes, pasta, chips, sweet glazes, and sugary dressings; avoid fried/breaded proteins, pizzas, noodle dishes, desserts, beer, and sweet cocktails.
