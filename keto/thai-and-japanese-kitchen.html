Keto at Thai and Japanese Kitchen: Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

65 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
Best keto choices at Thai and Japanese Kitchen are sashimi, hibachi proteins, hibachi dinners with no rice/noodles, and unsweetened drinks. Avoid nearly all rolls, ramen, udon/soba, tempura, katsu, dumplings, and sweet drinks because rice, noodles, breading, or sugar are central to the item.
