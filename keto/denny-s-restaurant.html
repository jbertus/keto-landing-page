Keto at Denny's Restaurant: Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

43 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
Denny's can work for keto if you build meals around eggs, bacon, sausage, ham, steak, bunless burgers, and salads with low-carb dressing. The main strategy is to remove toast, pancakes, hash browns, fries, buns, tortillas, fruit, sweet sauces, and croutons. Avoid breaded/fried items, pasta, fries/hash browns, desserts, smoothies, juice, lemonade, and milkshakes.
