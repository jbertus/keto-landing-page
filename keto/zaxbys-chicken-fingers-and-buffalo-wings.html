Keto at Zaxbys Chicken Fingers & Buffalo Wings: Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

22 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
Zaxby's is difficult for strict keto because most chicken items are breaded: Chicken Fingerz, boneless wings, Nibblerz, and most non-grilled sandwiches should be avoided. The best keto choices are traditional bone-in wings with Buffalo/dry-rub flavors, the Grilled Chicken Sandwich without the bun, and Zalads modified with grilled chicken, no toast/crunchy toppings, and low-carb dressing.
