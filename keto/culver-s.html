Keto at Culver's: Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

35 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
Culver's can work for keto if you focus on bunless ButterBurgers, the explicitly listed Grilled Chicken, Beef Pot Roast without the bun, salads with sweet/crunchy toppings removed, and low-carb sides like Steamed Broccoli or Garden Side Salad. Avoid the fried chicken, fried seafood, pork loin, cheese curds, fries, pretzel bites, corn dogs, custard, and sugary drinks. For value baskets, the entrée must be modified and the fries plus regular drink must be replaced with broccoli/salad and water or unsweetened coffee.
