Keto at LongHorn Steakhouse: Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

72 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
LongHorn can be very keto-friendly if you focus on steaks, grilled seafood, grilled chicken, ribs without glaze, and non-starchy sides like broccoli, asparagus, mushrooms, creamed spinach, or salad. Skip the complimentary bread, potatoes, rice, fries, mac & cheese, desserts, and sweet drinks; keep sauces and dressings on the side. The safest keto order is a plain steak or grilled seafood entrée with asparagus or broccoli.
