Keto at Whataburger: Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

49 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
Whataburger can work for keto if you treat burgers as bunless bowls or lettuce wraps and skip fries, Texas Toast, hash browns, biscuits, sugary sauces, and regular drinks. The safest choices are no-bun beef burgers, the grilled chicken sandwich without the bun, modified breakfast egg/sausage items, and salads with grilled chicken and low-carb dressing.
