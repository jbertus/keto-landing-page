Keto at Another Broken Egg Cafe: Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

40 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
Another Broken Egg Cafe can work for keto if you build around eggs, omelets, bacon, ham, sausage, Benedicts without the muffin, and sandwiches without bread or potatoes. Avoid pancakes, waffles, French toast, biscuits, grits, potatoes, gumbo, jambalaya, beignets, toast, milk, and fried/breaded chicken items. For a 7g net-carb meal cap, always say no English muffin, biscuit, toast, potatoes, grits, fruit, tortillas, honey mustard, and sweet toppings.
