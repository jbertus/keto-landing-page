Keto at Waffle House: Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

40 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
Waffle House can work well for keto if you build meals around eggs, bacon, sausage, ham, steak, pork chops, grilled chicken, and omelets. The main rule is to remove hashbrowns, grits, toast, biscuits, waffles, and sugary drinks; those are the carb sources that can quickly exceed a 12g net-carb meal limit.
