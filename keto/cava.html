Keto at CAVA: Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

16 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
CAVA can work for keto if you build a greens-only salad bowl with grilled chicken or grilled steak, feta/Crazy Feta, tzatziki, cucumber, olives, and dressing on the side. Avoid rice, lentils, pita, pita chips, corn, hummus, sweet drinks, desserts, and sweet proteins like harissa honey chicken or glazed salmon when staying under 7g net carbs per meal.

BUILD YOUR OWN
