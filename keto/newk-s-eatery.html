Keto at Newk's Eatery: Every Safe Order, With the Carbs Counted

61 diabetic-friendly picks • net carbs as modified • the orange lines are exactly what to say when you order.
Newk's is workable for keto if you convert sandwiches and wraps into lettuce bowls, remove bread/tortillas, skip BBQ or sweet sauces, and avoid chips, fruit, pasta salad, potato salad, pizza, mac and cheese, desserts, and sugary drinks. The safest choices are chicken salad without bread, deli-meat sandwich bowls, modified protein salads, Grab & Go proteins, low-carb dressings, water, and unsweet tea.
